Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how Prime Book Publishing Labs collects, manages, and protects your information when you visit www.primebookpublishinglabs.com. Protecting your privacy and maintaining the confidentiality of your personal information remains one of our highest priorities. This policy applies only to information collected through our website and outlines the following practices.

Information We Collect & How It Is Used

Prime Book Publishing Labs is the sole owner of the information collected through this website. We only receive information that you voluntarily provide through forms, emails, phone calls, or direct communication. Your information is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

The information collected is used strictly to respond to inquiries, deliver requested services, and improve your overall experience with our company. We do not share your personal information with outside parties unless it is necessary to complete a requested service.

Unless you request otherwise, we may occasionally contact you through phone or email regarding promotions, updates, new services, or changes to our policies.

Your Rights & Control Over Personal Information

You maintain full control over your personal information and may opt out of future communications at any time. You also have the right to:

  • Review the information we hold about you
  • Correct or update inaccurate details
  • Request deletion of your information from our systems
  • Raise questions or concerns regarding the use of your data

For any requests regarding your information, please contact us using the details available on our website.

Data Security & Protection

We follow strict security practices to keep your information protected both online and offline. Access to personally identifiable information is limited only to authorized employees or contractors responsible for tasks such as customer support, billing, or service fulfillment.

Our servers and systems are continuously monitored and maintained to help prevent unauthorized access and protect your personal data.

Prime Book Publishing Labs only collects the information necessary to provide requested services. All sensitive information is handled through secure and reliable systems designed to minimize security risks.

Secure Payment Processing

To process transactions and fulfill services, we may collect personal details including your name, email address, billing address, and phone number. All payment information and credit card processing are securely handled through trusted third-party payment providers.

IP Address Tracking & Cookies

Certain non-personal information, such as your IP address and browser activity, may automatically be recorded when visiting our website. This information is used to improve website performance, monitor traffic, and enhance user experience.

Our website may also use cookies to personalize your browsing experience and analyze visitor interaction across the website.

Prime Book Publishing Labs operates with complete confidentiality. Any content, files, drafts, or communications shared during collaboration remain private and are never disclosed to outside parties.

We do not claim ownership over any content, designs, or materials created for our clients. All completed work remains the exclusive property of the customer. By using our services, you remain recognized as the sole owner and creator of your content.

Originality & Authenticity

All content created by Prime Book Publishing Labs is developed with originality and authenticity in mind. We do not duplicate or reproduce material from external sources without proper acknowledgment.
