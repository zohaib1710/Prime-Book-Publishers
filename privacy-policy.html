Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

About The Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices and measures implemented by Prime Book Publishers to safeguard your privacy when you visit our website, www.primebookpublishers.com. We are deeply committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring the confidentiality of the services we provide. This policy applies only to data collected on our website and addresses the following elements:

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

We at Prime Book Publishers are the sole owners of the data we collect on our site. We only have access to information you provide us with your consent, either via email or by directly contacting us. We will not rent or sell your data to any third party.

We use your data to respond to your requests and to fulfill the reason you contacted us. Your personal information is not shared with outside parties unless required to fulfill your specific needs.

Unless you specifically tell us otherwise, we might periodically contact you via telephone or email to inform you of our current specials or new services. We may also notify you of revisions to our privacy policy.

Your Access to and Control Over Information

You have complete control over your personal information, and you can decide to stop receiving future communications from us at any time. To exercise these rights, you can:

  • Examine the information we have about you, if any.
  • Correct or amend any errors in your information.
  • Request the removal of your data from our databases.
  • Raise any concerns about how we use your data.

If you have any questions about these actions, please contact us via the email address or telephone number listed on our website.

Security

We have taken strict steps to ensure that your data is safe offline. Personally identifiable information is only granted to contractors and employees who require it to perform specific tasks like billing or fulfilling services. Our servers are secured and maintained to protect the security of your data.

Prime Book Publishers values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. We only collect the details required to fulfill your order and do not share any of your personal information without your express consent. Our data collection practices are carried out using highly secure platforms to prevent data breaches.

Payment

To fulfill your order, we gather personal information like your name, email address, billing address, and phone number. Be aware that credit card information is only handled by our third-party credit card companies to ensure security.

IP Address and Cookies

Non-personal information, like the IP address of your computer, is automatically recorded whenever you visit our site. This information is only used for diagnosing issues with servers and managing the site. Cookies are utilized on our website to improve user experience and collect information about usage.

Information About Our Services and Products

We make every effort to provide accurate descriptions of the services and products offered on www.primebookpublishers.com. However, we cannot guarantee that the product or service descriptions or any other content on our website are accurate, complete, reliable, or up-to-date. If you believe that the product or service you received differs from the descriptions on our website, please promptly contact us via phone +1 (407) 680-0272 or email at info@primebookpublishinglabs.com.

Confidentiality and Ownership

Prime Book Publishers operates with complete confidentiality. All content or communications created or reviewed for collaboration purposes will not be divulged to third parties under any circumstances. We claim no rights over the products or services we offer. Any material we write or edit is acknowledged as the exclusive property of the customer. When you utilize our services, only you are recognized as the creator and/or owner of the content.

Originality

All original content created or written by Prime Book Publishers for clients will be original and free of plagiarism. It will never be copied from another source. Any information derived from external sources will be properly cited to preserve the authenticity of the content.

If you have any concerns or questions about this privacy policy or believe that Prime Book Publishers is not adhering to it, please contact us immediately via telephone at (407) 680-0272 or email us at info@primebookpublishinglabs.com. We will ensure the absolute protection of your privacy and the confidentiality of your personal information.
