Frequently Asked Questions

Humans make mistakes. Writers aren't perfect. In fact, they're vulnerable to mistakes as the process of writing a novel is exhausting. When you're defining the character arcs of your characters, laying out plot lines and constructing an imaginary world it is easy to lose your focus and commit mistakes in your writing and consistency. Even non-fictional writers who must conduct extensive research, often end up using incorrect information. Hiring a professional editor can be an efficient way of making sure that your work is error-free.

On average, manuscript editing services charge between $1000 and $3500 when editing and proofreading books. The final cost is contingent upon the volume of the book that's why editors charge per word as well as per page basis.

It may take between 7-10 business days editing a book that has under 50 pages. A book with between 50 and 200 pages can take between 10 and 13 days. These are only estimates since editing is a bespoke service.

Prime Book Publishing Labs provides book editing services for any genre. You may need reviews on romance novels, or are looking to determine an authenticity assessment of a book, there's an editorial team which can finish the job.

Even when you're an author with numerous best-selling titles to his name, it's not advised to publish your book without an editor. Since a book contains numerous elements that are in motion, the book should be reviewed by several people to ensure everything is in place. Professional editors have the expertise and experience to turn manuscripts into exciting and engaging books.
