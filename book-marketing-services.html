Frequently Asked Questions

Publishing a book and marketing the book are two different tasks. As a writer you don't have to be aware of the strategies that can bring your book the attention you want. Book marketers who are professionals have the knowledge and expertise of promoting books across multiple platforms.

eBook marketing requires an approach that is multi-channel to boost the number of sales for your electronic books. It is important to increase the book's position on various publishing platforms, and then promote consistently on social media and emails. A lot of eBook marketing solutions use affiliate marketing, in which a person promotes the book via their website or blog and earns a percentage of each sale.

Book marketing services have specialists who are part of online reading communities. They also have emails of readers most likely to purchase your book. With these experts working with you significantly boosts the visibility of your book, allowing you to increase sales.

It's not easy to estimate the price of publishing books because they are influenced by many elements. If you partner with a professional to receive advice and help with book publishing, the expense is minimal. But, having professionals create a cover, modify the content and create the format of your book will undoubtedly increase the price.

Book marketers can identify potential readers of a book, and determine the most effective mediums to market the book. They devise strategies to increase the sales of the book, and make the work to promote the writer's image.

Self-published authors are likely to invest between $100 and 4000 dollars on marketing, based on how they wish to present their work. Book marketing must be viewed as a long-term commitment because an effective marketing strategy for your manuscript can establish your name as an established author in a specific field. This will naturally boost sales of your subsequent books.
